Egg Muffin Sandwich

The loved and adored egg muffin sandwich but homemade, and who doesn't love homemade everything? You can add any topping you like! Add Jalapenos for a kick. Have fun with it, that's all that cooking is about. This will make 2 muffins.

Step 1.

Cut the english muffins in half.

Step 2.

Put the 2 eggs in a cup, add a tablespoon of milk and some salt and pepper to taste. Put in the mircowave for 2 minutes. Take out, flip upside down and cut in half.

Step 3.

Butter the muffin to your preference.

Step 4.

Start piling the veggies on top first if you're having any. If not place then ham or bacon, then the cheese and on top the cooked egg.

Step 5.

Place your top bun on top and ENJOY!!
    Everything you'll need:
  • 2 English muffins (this can be swapped for crumpets/bread rolls)
  • 2 Large eggs
  • A tablespoon of milk
  • Cheese (can be replaced by vegan cheese)
  • Ham or bacon (optional)
  • Spinach / Lettuce any other veggies (optional)
  • Salt or pepper to taste
  • Butter to go on the muffin
